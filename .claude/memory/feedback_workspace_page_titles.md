In the workspace, all page titles that appear after `+++` must be rendered in Title Case (e.g. "My Workspace Page", not "my workspace page" or "My workspace page").

**Why:** User preference for consistent visual hierarchy and presentation in the workspace section of Vector PM.

**How to apply:** When creating or editing any workspace page where the title appears after the `+++` marker, ensure the title is title-cased. Capitalise principal words; keep articles, conjunctions, and short prepositions lowercase unless they begin the title.
